Anxiety

How to Help

Do's and Dont's of Anxiety
Do: Don't:
  • Learn about the disorder
  • Stay with the person and keep calm
  • Listen more than talk. Really listen closely to understand how they're feeling
  • Spend time with them as much as possible
  • Tell them to call you anytime,anywhere
  • Do exciting activities
  • Be proud of them when they improve
  • Be yourself
  • Reassure them that they're goung to be okay
  • Encourage them to seek professional help
  • Be available
  • Be patient
  • Affirm them
  • Realize and accept stressful periods
  • Continually offer hope
  • Dont assume that they're anxious about something specific
  • Don't tell them to get over it
  • Don't force them to do anything
  • Don't get frustrated
  • Don't be discouraged
  • Don't bring up the anxiety often
  • Don't let anxiety affect you as well
  • Don't expect immidiate, massive turnarounds
  • Don't guilt trip
  • Don't give up hope
  • Don't stay away or avoid the person
  • Don't minimize what they're going through
  • Don't make negative comments
  • Don't be judgemental

Anxiety

Signs

These are signs of Anxiety to look for in a teenager:

  • Stressed out all the time
  • Experiencing sleep difficulties
  • Irrational fears
  • Chronic digestive problems
  • Suffer from panic attacks
  • Excessive worry
  • Muscle tension
  • Stage fright
  • Compulsive behaviors
  • Self-doubt
  • Flashbacks
  • Quick breathing
  • Wound up and edgy
  • Shortness of breath
  • Shaking and trembling
  • Frequent urination or diarrhea
  • Upset stomach

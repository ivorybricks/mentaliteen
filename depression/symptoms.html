Depression

Symptoms

If someone has Depression, they may experience some of these symptoms:

  • Lack of ability to concentrate and make decisions.
  • Overeating and weight gain or loss of appetite and weight loss (or in teens, not gaining weight as expected while growing.)
  • Restlessness or excessive sleeping and tiredness.
  • Feelings of guilt or blaming oneself for everything.
  • Fatigue; aches, pains and sickness that have no apparent cause; frustration, irritability, and a hard time concentrating
  • Feelings of sadness; an inability to feel pleasure or no longer taking interest in favorite activities and hobbies,sad mood and/or loss of interest in life.
  • Feelings of hopelessness and despair; loneliness; and thoughts of death or suicide.
  • Lacking in confidence
  • Indecisive
  • Frustrated

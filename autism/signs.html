Autism

Signs

These are signs of Autism to look for in a teenager:

  • Behing in speach. Babbles or has a speach impedement.
  • Gets stuck in a loop. Let's say you're in a conversation and you notice that once you've changed the subject, the person who you're talking with gets stuck on the same subject from before.
  • Have a difficult time communicating with new groups of people or in public.
  • Not able to regulate emotions
  • Must have things done in a specific way.
  • Seizures. This is just something commonly found in teens with autism but not typical.
  • Hypersensitive to sound or touch
  • Difficulty for doing things socially

ADHD

How to Help

Do: Don't:
  • Advocate for teen’s educational needs
  • Encourage teen to succeed
  • Allow teen time to respond to commitments to prevent impulsive decisions
  • If angry, leave the room and take a few deep breaths before trying to communicate
  • Reward positive behavior
  • Be consistent
  • Control your own behavior to be a good role model
  • Give gentle reminders
  • Break goals into smaller and more easily manageable chunks
  • Give positive feedback
  • Ask teen if s/he wants help scheduling or organizing school work
  • If possible, avoid conversations in busy and loud areas
  • Don’t resent child for being different
  • Don’t treat medication as the only option
  • Don’t set unrealistic goals
  • Do not yell at teen for forgetting a task
  • Do not speak to the teen in a negative way (this will only lessen motivation and self-esteem)
  • Do not ignore positive behavior
  • Don’t yell

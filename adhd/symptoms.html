ADHD

Symptoms

If someone has ADHD, they may experience some of these symptoms:

  • Forgetful
  • Easily distracted
  • Fidgeting
  • Restlessness
  • Short attention span for age
  • Trouble organizing taks and activities
  • Forgetful
  • Fails to give close attention to details
  • Makes careless mistakes in schoolwork, at work, or with other activities
  • Talks excessively
  • Lack of restraint
  • Anger/irritability
  • Anxiety
  • Boredom
  • Impulsivity
